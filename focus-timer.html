Pomodoro technique

  • Pick one task before you start; protect the full block from interruptions.
  • Default rhythm: 25 min focus → 5 min short break; every fourth break, take a 15 min long break.
  • When the timer ends, stand, stretch, hydrate—then reset or switch to break mode.
